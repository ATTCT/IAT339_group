Bar Candy

BAR CANDY® creator Liz Green was a bartender in Los Angeles and started experimenting with flavors behind the bar. Before too long she was winning cocktail competitions and liquor companies were hiring her to create cocktails for their brands. She then created menus for restaurants, bars and nightclubs, and authored an original cocktail book called Cocktail Hollywood.

She ran a series of nightclubs in New York, and after realizing that the cocktail craze wasn’t going anywhere, she decided to create an experience. It was at this time that Bar Candy® was born! FUN FACT: BAR CANDY® made it to the final round of Shark Tank beating out thousands of people. It came down to BAR CANDY® and another brand!

BAR CANDY® Edible Glasses are made from Belgian Chocolate without additives or preservatives. This patented item was developed by award winning mixologist Liz Green and was included in Martha Stewart TOP 5 BEST WEDDING TRENDS on Fox news, nominated twice Best New Product (Bizbash), and has garnered the attention of Colin Cowie, Bobby Flay and Kim Kardashian, to name a few.

BAR CANDY® is packaged in quantities of 48 and can stand at room temperature for about 15 hours without melting, (out of direct heat), and refrigeration for up to 6 months and frozen for 12 months. BAR CANDY® can be ordered in a lesser quantity. Contact us for details BAR CANDY® can be enjoyed with Champagne, Specialty Cocktails, Mocktails, and Desserts. Flavors include, Dark, White, White with Swirls of any color, Dark with White Swirls. They can be rimmed with edible glitter, cookie bits, candy, sprinkles, etc. Use your imagination to create a BAR CANDY® experience! Match colors, use garnishes, and create drinks and desserts to make it your own! Creating a sustainable product was at the top of the priority list when we created BAR CANDY®. The holders can be retained and used again, and the chocolate cups can be used for drinks without the holders and a customized sleeve is used. Glass holders are also available for restaurants and high end weddings. Contact Us for details. Kosher available.
