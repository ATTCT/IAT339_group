Frequently Asked Questions

Do the glasses (cones) hold drinks and champagne for a long time?

Yes. The glasses can hold liquids and desserts for several hours at room temperature. Please do notuse in the heat and and hot weather outside.

Can I store them?

Yes. They can be stored at room temperature for 24-48 hours. After this they should be refrigerated orfrozen. They can last for weeks and months when frozen. Please try and use at room temperature as thisis when the chocolate is the most fresh. We do not add preservatives.

How high is the quality of chocolate?

The chocolate is the highest quality Belgian chocolate. No additives. Natural ingredients with a high cocoa butter percentage for a shiny stunning visual. The chocolate is also very fragrant and will fill the are with a delightful aroma

I don’t drink alcohol. Can I still use Bar Candy?

Absolutely! We have several suggestions for non alcoholic mocktails. Award winning mixologists create these. We offer one free original recipe per order. The glasses also hold desserts.

Do you offer personalization?

Yes. We can ship you personalized clear stickers to adhere to the glass/plastic holders. Or we can make dough discs to adhere to the chocolate cones for an extra fee. We can also make personalized chocolate discs with your name or company name and use as a coaster for under the glasses!

Can I customize and personalize the gift sets?

Absolutely! We offer custom products and personalized ribbon for your name, or name of your company.
